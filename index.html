A marketer who ships.

I'm a T-shaped marketer — depth in B2B/SaaS growth, communication, and lifecycle; breadth across product, applied AI, GTM automation, and shipping the web surfaces myself. In practice that means I don't hand a brief to a developer and wait for a website — I open Claude Code and ship one. I don't ask an SDR to source leads — I build the agentic flow that does it. I don't write a positioning doc and pray someone reads it — I publish it as a comparison page that AI answer engines cite by name.

I'm co-founder and CEO of Nautilida, an AI-native sales platform. I lead product strategy and go-to-market end-to-end alongside a technical co-founder: roadmap, positioning, messaging, customer development. I secured our first LOIs and onboarded our first design partner. I designed the site, wrote every word of it, set up the GTM engine, and operate the lead pipeline. Before Nautilida, I owned user communication at Dataiku (an enterprise AI/analytics platform) — building email and in-product messaging for the freemium-to-paid lifecycle across two distinct user segments.

What I'm looking for next: fractional Head of Growth engagements, founding marketer roles at AI-native startups, or projects where I get to design, ship, and operate — not just brief and review.
